Climate Change Awareness

Together, we can protect our planet for future generations.

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to significant, long-term changes in the Earth's climate patterns, largely driven by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and pollution.

Why Should We Care?

  • Rising sea levels threaten coastal communities.
  • Extreme weather events are becoming more frequent.
  • Biodiversity loss impacts food security and health.
  • Climate change strains water supplies and agriculture.

What Can You Do?

  • Conserve energy and use renewable sources when possible.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste.
  • Eat locally and reduce food waste.
  • Raise awareness and support environmental policies.
